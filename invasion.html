Invasion PDX

Invasion PDX

Film, motion, and brand design for a Portland-based music festival concept. Invasion PDX is a week long celebration of hip-hop and underground rap culture in the underbelly of Portland's metro area.
The art direction highlights the underground energy and motion of rap and hip hop culture. The intention is to give a raw look at the music from the audience's perspective.
The logo mark is an abstraction of the INVASION title, inspired by secret codes and grafitti tagging. The logo is adaptive to work on video, signage, apparel, and more to bring all event attendees under the same banner with this invasion of sound and music culture.
