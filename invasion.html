Invasion PDX

Film, motion, and brand design for a Portland-based music festival concept. Letting music take the city for a week long celebration of hip-hop and underground rap culture.

The mark is an abstraction of the INVASION title, using a bold, glyphic design, reminiscent of streetwear design. The logo is adaptive to work on video, signage, apparel, and more to bring all event attendees under the same banner with this invasion of sound and music culture.
