Digital Landscapes

A projection mapping installation for Portland State Graphic Design, done for the college president's visit. The work explores the connections of art and technology, and the limitless imagination of design students in our digital generation.

The animation is projected onto two angled pillars from one stationary projector across the room. Clouds, airplanes, and floating cubes are then masked on the wall behind, allowing the landscapes to bleed out into the space and drift across the room.
