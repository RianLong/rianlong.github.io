Rian Long is a digital and experiential designer based out of Portland, Oregon. He is a graphic design graduate from Portland State University, with a focus on digital and interactive design. His work spans identities, interfaces, experiences, installations, and animations. Overall his aim is to build immersive design-driven experiences that evolve from the screen to the physical space.

Feel free to reach out with inquiries and collaborations.
