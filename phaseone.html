Look Up: PHASE 1

PHASE 1 was the beginning of a multi-part projection installation series. The pop-up experience took place in downtown Portland, showing a collection of visual and audio experiments to a public audience.

The Look Up campaign focused on bridging art and technology in an exciting urban experience. The campaign included a promotional website, social media advertisements, and an email newsletter. LookUpPDX.com serves as a sneak preview for the visual style and a landing page to learn more about the upcoming Look Up events.

Phase 1 was the setup and proof of concept for the upcoming Look Up: Phase 2 experience. Phase 2 will feature interactive installations with an exclusive nightlife event. Tests are already underway on camera-tracking effects, projection photobooths, and light grafitti installations.
