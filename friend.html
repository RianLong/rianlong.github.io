F is for Friend

A fully designed and developed web-based, interactive adventure game, where the player navigates a functional operating system, while battling with a vengeful artifical intelligence.

'F' is an AI chat bot, who watches over the operating system, keeping the player in line. It uses a fully custom chat bot script, with 50+ unique responses and the ability to send photos, open menus, and take control of the interface manually.

The operating system includes a custom coded, sortable file system, with folders, images, videos, and text documents. It also has a web portal to navigate through integrated websites and unlock clues on how to take down 'F' for good.
