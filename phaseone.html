Look Up Phase 1

Look Up Phase 1

The Look Up projection series focuses on bridging art and technology in an exciting urban experience. PHASE 1 was the launch of a multi-part installation. The outdoor projection took place in downtown Portland, showing a collection of visual and audio experiments to a public audience.
The campaign included a promotional website, social media advertisements, and an email newsletter. LookUpPDX.com serves as a sneak preview for the visual style and a landing page to learn more about the upcoming Look Up events.
Phase 1 was the setup and proof of concept for the upcoming Look Up: Phase 2 experience. Phase 2 will feature interactive installations with an exclusive nightlife event. Tests are already underway on camera-tracking effects, projection photo booths, and light graffiti installations.
