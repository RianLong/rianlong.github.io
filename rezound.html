Rezound

Rezound

A collaborative party music app concept, allowing users to control all of the tunes from one device, take suggestions, and vote on their favorites for an audience generated playlist.
User experience design was considered in onboarding users and naturally teaching them the features of the app. The workflow for creating a room follows a series of quick customization questions, linked with a progress bar and review screen. The room view also features both button and gesture controls to allow for navigation and wayfinding between screens.
